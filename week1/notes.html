Master Mobile UX

Rewire your brain for mobile first

  • Responsive Retrofitting is taking what you have already and making it work on mobile. The pros are: its cheaper,faster than ground rewrite. Results in better mobile experience than only desktop. The cons are: it doesn't address screen real estate and touch interaction issues.
  • Mobile First is going mobile to desktop.The cons are: time-consuming, costly for full rebuild. The pros: best experience across many devices, deals with constraints for size/bandwith, future-friendly, adaptable/growth.

Summary: Desktop sales have been in decline for the past decade, people are on their phones not their labtops, start small & scale up, Mobile is sticking around for awhile

Improve UX through Users

Know the users better than themselves. Focus less on what they say and more on what they are doing.

  1. Be there: Users expect you to be there to help them.
  2. Be useful: Don't make users hunt for what they want.
  3. Be quick: Avoid excessive steps causing user frustration.

Reachability is making sure key items are in easy to reach places.

Boost speed by:

  1. Reducing image usage
  2. Use SVG's & Webfonts
  3. Optimize & Minify CSS and JS files
  4. GZIP (compress those files) to remove extras

Use Strategic Menu designs

Put primary controls and menu at the bottom

If you can place things correctly you can fix & improve reachability, speed, engagement & user satisfaction.

Mobile forms
